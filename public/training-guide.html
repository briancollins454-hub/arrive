Chapter 01

Getting Started

Arrivé is a cloud-based Property Management System designed for boutique hotels. Access it from any modern browser — no installation required. Your property manager will provide your login credentials.

🔑 Logging In
Navigate to the login page
Open your browser and go to www.arrivebooking.online/login (or your custom property URL).
Select your property
Choose your hotel from the property selector if multiple properties are configured.
Enter your credentials
Enter your staff email and PIN code provided by your manager. Click Sign In.
You're in!
You'll be taken to the Dashboard. The sidebar menu shows all modules available to your role.
💡 Tip
Bookmark the login page for quick access. Arrivé works on tablets and phones too — perfect for housekeeping staff on the go.
Chapter 02

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your command centre. At a glance, you can see today's arrivals, departures, occupancy, revenue, and any items that need attention.

📊 Key Metrics

Today's occupancy rate, ADR (Average Daily Rate), RevPAR, total revenue, and number of rooms available — all updated in real time.

🛬 Today's Arrivals

A list of all guests checking in today with their confirmation code, room assignment, and special requests. Click any arrival to open the booking detail.

🛫 Today's Departures

Guests checking out today. Quickly verify balances and process check-outs directly from the dashboard cards.

⏳ In-House Guests

See everyone currently staying at the property with their room number, remaining nights, and any outstanding balance.

🔔 Action Items

Overdue housekeeping tasks, unassigned rooms, pending payments, and maintenance requests that need your attention.

📈 Revenue Chart

Visual breakdown of revenue over the past 7 or 30 days, segmented by room revenue, extras, and booking sources.

📋 Sidebar Navigation

The left sidebar contains all modules. Your visible options depend on your staff role. Key sections include:

  • Dashboard — Home overview with KPIs
  • Calendar — Visual booking calendar
  • Bookings — All reservations list
  • In-House — Currently staying guests
  • Guests — Guest database & profiles
  • Rooms — Room inventory & status
  • Housekeeping — Cleaning tasks & schedules
  • Payments — Payment terminal
  • Rates — Pricing & rate periods
  • Reports — Operational & financial reports
  • Settings — Property configuration
Chapter 03

Bookings — Creating, Editing & Managing

Bookings are the core of Arrivé. Every reservation tracks the guest, dates, room type, rate, payments, and status throughout the entire stay lifecycle.

➕ Creating a New Booking
Open Bookings page
Navigate to Bookings in the sidebar. Click the + New Booking button.
Select or create a guest
Search for an existing guest by name/email, or create a new guest profile inline. Fill in first name, last name, email, and phone.
Set dates and room type
Pick check-in and check-out dates. Select a room type — the system shows available rooms and the current rate.
Choose booking source
Select how the booking was made: Direct, Phone, Walk-In, Booking.com, Expedia, Airbnb, Travel Agent, Corporate, etc.
Add special requests & notes
Record any guest preferences, dietary requirements, or special occasions in the notes field.
Confirm & save
Review the summary and click Create Booking. A unique confirmation code is generated automatically.
🔍 Booking Statuses
StatusMeaningNext Action
Pending Booking created but not yet confirmed Confirm or cancel
Confirmed Reservation is confirmed and guaranteed Assign room, await arrival
Checked In Guest has arrived and is in-house Manage stay, post charges
Checked Out Guest has departed, folio settled Archive / review
Cancelled Reservation was cancelled Record reason, process refund if needed
No Show Guest did not arrive on check-in date Mark no-show, apply policy
📝 Booking Detail Page

Click any booking to open its detail page. From here you can:

  • View the full guest profile and contact details
  • See assigned room and room type with rate information
  • Manage the Guest Folio — view charges, add payments, post extras
  • Process check-in and check-out
  • Change room assignment or extend/shorten the stay
  • Add internal notes visible only to staff
  • View the activity log for a full audit trail
  • Cancel the booking with a reason
Chapter 04

The Booking Lifecycle

Every booking follows a natural journey from creation to departure. Understanding this lifecycle ensures smooth operations and happy guests.

✅ Check-In Process
Find the booking
Use Today's Arrivals on the Dashboard, or search in Bookings by guest name or confirmation code.
Verify guest identity
Confirm the guest's name and booking details. Check any special requests or notes.
Assign a room (if not already assigned)
Select an available room of the correct type. The system prevents assigning rooms that are occupied or in maintenance.
Collect payment or verify deposit
If payment is due, use the + Payment button to process a card payment or record cash/transfer.
Click "Check In"
The booking status changes to Checked In. The room status automatically updates to Occupied.
ℹ️ Room Status Sync
When you check a guest in, the room automatically moves to "Occupied." When you check them out, it returns to "Available" (or "Dirty" if housekeeping is enabled). You never need to manually change room status for check-ins/outs.
🛫 Check-Out Process
Open the booking
Find the guest in Today's Departures on the Dashboard or via the In-House page.
Review the folio
Check all charges are correct — room charges, minibar, restaurant, etc. Ensure the balance is zero or collect the outstanding amount.
Settle any remaining balance
Process final payment via card, cash, or transfer. The guest should have a zero balance before departure.
Click "Check Out"
The booking moves to Checked Out. The room is released and becomes available for the next guest.
⚠️ Important
Always settle the folio before checking out. If a guest needs to be checked out with a remaining balance (e.g., corporate account), record it as a City Ledger transfer first.
Chapter 05

Calendar & Tape Chart

Visual tools for seeing your property's availability at a glance — essential for managing occupancy and spotting gaps.

📅 Booking Calendar

A month-view calendar showing all bookings as coloured bars. Colour-coded by status: blue for confirmed, green for checked-in, grey for checked-out. Click any booking bar to jump to its detail page. Use the arrow buttons to navigate between months.

📊 Tape Chart

A grid view showing rooms on the Y-axis and dates on the X-axis. Each cell shows occupancy. Perfect for seeing exactly which physical rooms are available on which dates. Drag to select dates and create new bookings directly. Colour-coded by room status.

💡 Tip
Use the Tape Chart when a guest asks "Do you have a specific room available next week?" — it shows availability per physical room, not just per room type.
Chapter 06

Rooms & Room Management

Manage your physical room inventory, room types, and real-time room statuses. The system prevents double-booking at the database level.

🚪 Room Statuses
✓
Available
●
Occupied
🔧
Maintenance
✕
Blocked
  • Available — Clean and ready for the next guest
  • Occupied — Currently has a checked-in guest (set automatically)
  • Maintenance — Out of order for repairs; not bookable
  • Blocked — Manually held out of inventory (e.g., owner use, renovation)
🏷️ Room Types

Room types define categories like "Standard Double," "Deluxe King," or "Family Suite." Each room type has:

  • Base nightly rate
  • Maximum occupancy
  • Amenities list (WiFi, TV, minibar, sea view, etc.)
  • Bed configuration (king, twin, double, etc.)
  • Photos displayed on the booking engine
🛑 Double-Booking Protection
Arrivé enforces an exclusion constraint at the database level. It is physically impossible to assign two overlapping bookings to the same room. If you try, the system will reject it with an error.
Chapter 07

Guest Management

Build a rich guest database over time. Track preferences, stay history, and spending to deliver personalised service.

👤 Guest Profiles

Each guest profile stores:

  • Contact info — Name, email, phone, nationality
  • Preferences — Dietary requirements, room preferences, allergies
  • Stay history — Total stays, total spend, all past bookings
  • Tags — Custom labels like "VIP," "Loyalty Member," "Corporate," "Returner"
  • Notes — Free-text notes visible to all staff
🔍 Finding Guests

On the Guests page, you can:

  • Search by name, email, or phone number
  • Filter by tags (e.g., show all VIP guests)
  • Sort by total stays or total spend to identify your best customers
  • Click a guest to view their full profile and booking history
💡 Tip
When a returning guest calls, search for them by name. Their preferences and past stay details help you offer personalised service — "Welcome back, Mrs. Johnson! Would you like Room 204 again with extra pillows?"
Chapter 08

Payments & Folios

Every booking has a folio — a running account of charges and payments. Arrivé supports card payments via Stripe, plus cash and transfer recording.

💳 Processing Payments

From the Booking Detail page, click + Payment to open the payment dialog:

MethodHow It Works
Card Processed securely via Stripe. Guest enters card details in the secure payment form. Funds are captured immediately.
Cash Record the amount received. Enter a reference (e.g., receipt number). The payment is logged to the folio.
Bank Transfer Record a transfer payment with a bank reference number for reconciliation.
Online For payments made through OTAs or external systems. Record with a transaction reference.
📑 Guest Folio

The folio tab on each booking shows:

  • Room charges — Automatically posted nightly room rate
  • Extras / Add-ons — Minibar, restaurant, laundry, etc. (manually posted by staff)
  • Payments received — All payments with method, amount, and timestamp
  • Running balance — Total charges minus total payments
  • Full Balance button — Quickly set the payment amount to the outstanding balance
🖥️ Payment Terminal

The dedicated Payments page in the sidebar provides a standalone payment terminal for processing payments outside of a specific booking — useful for walk-in purchases, gift cards, or deposits.

🏛️ City Ledger

The City Ledger tracks outstanding balances for corporate accounts, travel agents, or guests who have checked out with an unpaid balance. Transfer charges here during check-out for later invoicing.

Chapter 09

Housekeeping

Keep rooms clean and ready. The housekeeping module provides a real-time task board that updates as guests check in and out.

🧹 Housekeeping Board

The Housekeeping page shows all rooms with their cleaning status:

  • Clean — Room is inspected and guest-ready
  • Dirty — Needs cleaning (auto-set after check-out)
  • In Progress — Housekeeper is currently cleaning
  • Inspected — Cleaned and verified by supervisor
📱 For Housekeeping Staff
Log in on your phone or tablet
Use your housekeeping PIN to log in. You'll see only the Housekeeping module based on your role.
View your assigned rooms
See which rooms are marked "Dirty" and need attention. Prioritise by departure/arrival times shown.
Mark as "In Progress" when you start
Tap the room and change status. Front desk can see in real time that the room is being cleaned.
Mark as "Clean" when done
The room becomes available for the next check-in. Add notes if anything needs attention (e.g., "lightbulb needs replacing").
Chapter 10

Rates & Packages

Flexible pricing tools let you set seasonal rates, create special offers, and bundle rooms with extras.

💰 Rate Periods

The Rates page lets you set pricing that varies by season or event:

  • Base rate — Default nightly rate set on each room type
  • Rate periods — Override pricing for date ranges (e.g., "Summer Peak," "Christmas," "Festival")
  • Per room type or all — A rate period can apply to one room type or all types
  • Minimum stay — Enforce minimum nights during busy periods
🎁 Packages

The Packages page lets you create bundled offers:

  • Combine a room with extras (e.g., "Romance Package" = Deluxe Room + Champagne + Late Checkout)
  • Set a package price that differs from the sum of components
  • Packages appear on the guest booking engine as special offers
🤖 Rate Intelligence

The Rate Intelligence page provides AI-powered pricing suggestions based on:

  • Current occupancy levels and demand patterns
  • Competitor pricing in your area
  • Historical booking data and trends
  • Upcoming events and holidays
Chapter 11

Night Audit

The night audit is a daily end-of-day procedure that closes the business day, posts room charges, and generates daily reports.

🌙 Running the Night Audit
Navigate to Night Audit
Go to Night Audit in the sidebar. The page shows a pre-audit checklist.
Review checklist items
Verify: all arrivals processed, departures checked out, no-shows marked, all folios balanced.
Post room charges
The system posts tonight's room charge to every in-house guest's folio automatically.
Close the day
Click Run Night Audit. The system advances the business date and generates the daily summary report.
⚠️ Important
The night audit should be run once daily, typically after midnight by the night shift or early morning by the first shift. It cannot be undone — ensure all corrections are made before running it.
Chapter 12

Reports & Financials

Comprehensive reporting tools for tracking performance, revenue, and operations.

📊 Reports Page

Generate reports including: Occupancy Report, Revenue Report, Booking Sources, Guest Statistics, Arrivals/Departures, No-Show Report, Cancellation Report, and more. Filter by date range and export data.

💹 Financial Dashboard

Real-time financial overview: total revenue, average daily rate (ADR), RevPAR, accounts receivable, payment method breakdown, revenue by room type, and month-over-month comparisons.

🤖 AI Insights

AI-powered analytics that surface trends, anomalies, and opportunities. Get suggestions for pricing adjustments, staffing optimisation, and upsell opportunities.

📋 Activity Log

Complete audit trail of every action taken in the system: bookings created, payments processed, status changes, staff logins. Filter by staff member, action type, or date.

ℹ️ Key Metrics Explained
ADR (Average Daily Rate) = Total Room Revenue ÷ Rooms Sold
RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room) = Total Room Revenue ÷ Total Available Rooms
Occupancy % = Rooms Sold ÷ Total Available Rooms × 100

Additional Modules

Arrivé includes specialised modules for complete hotel operations management.

🔧 Maintenance

Log and track maintenance requests. Assign tasks to maintenance staff, set priorities (urgent/high/medium/low), and track resolution. Link issues to specific rooms.

📦 Lost & Found

Track items found by housekeeping or reported lost by guests. Record item descriptions, location found, and link to guest profiles for returns.

🛎️ Concierge

Manage guest requests, restaurant reservations, tour bookings, transportation, and special arrangements. Track fulfillment status for each request.

👥 Group Bookings

Handle group reservations for weddings, conferences, or tour groups. Manage multiple rooms under one master booking with group billing.

📡 Channel Manager

Manage your distribution across OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia, Airbnb, etc.). Sync availability and rates across all channels from one place.

⏰ Staff Rota

Schedule staff shifts, manage rotas, and ensure adequate coverage across all departments. View weekly/monthly schedules.

📝 Waitlist

When fully booked, add guests to a waitlist. Automatically notify them when a room becomes available due to cancellation.

🏛️ City Ledger

Track outstanding balances for corporate accounts and travel agents. Generate invoices and reconcile payments.

Chapter 15

Staff Roles & Permissions

Arrivé uses role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure staff only see what they need. Over 70 granular permissions are available.

Full Access

Owner

Complete access to all modules, settings, and staff management. Can view financial reports, modify rates, manage properties, and configure the system. The only role that can manage other staff accounts.

High Access

Manager

Access to most operational modules: bookings, guests, rooms, payments, housekeeping, reports, and rates. Can run night audits and view financials. Cannot modify system settings or manage staff.

Operational Access

Receptionist

Front desk operations: create and manage bookings, check guests in/out, process payments, view room status, and access guest profiles. Cannot view financial reports or modify rates.

Limited Access

Housekeeping

View and update room cleaning status only. Can mark rooms as clean/dirty/in-progress. Cannot access bookings, guest details, payments, or any other module.

ℹ️ Permission Examples
The system checks permissions like bookings.view, payments.process, rooms.edit, reports.view, settings.edit. If a staff member doesn't have a required permission, the menu item is hidden and the page is inaccessible.
Chapter 16

Guest Booking Engine

Arrivé includes a beautiful, guest-facing booking engine. Guests can browse rooms, select dates, and book directly — commission-free.

🌐 How It Works

The booking engine is accessed at /book/your-hotel-slug — each property has a unique URL based on its slug. The guest journey:

Hotel Landing Page
Shows your hotel's branding, description, photos, and a date picker for checking availability.
Room Selection
Available room types displayed with photos, amenities, bed config, and pricing. Guest picks their room.
Checkout
Guest enters their details and processes payment securely via Stripe. Tax is calculated automatically.
Confirmation
Booking confirmation page with code, dates, and property details. Confirmation email sent automatically.
Manage Booking
Guests can look up their booking with confirmation code and email to view details or request changes.
💡 Tip
Share your booking engine URL on your website, social media, and Google Business listing. Direct bookings mean no OTA commission — typically saving 15-20% per booking.
